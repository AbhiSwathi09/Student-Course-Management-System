Introduction to Cybersecurity

Understand the fundamentals of cybersecurity, protect yourself from cyber threats, and explore a career in digital defense.

Instructor: Dr. Michael Chen

Enroll Now for Free!

Course Overview

In an increasingly connected world, understanding cybersecurity is no longer just for IT professionals—it's essential for everyone. This introductory course provides a comprehensive overview of the core concepts, threats, and defense mechanisms in cybersecurity. You'll learn how to identify common cyber risks, implement basic protective measures for your personal and professional digital life, and gain insight into the various roles and career paths within the cybersecurity industry.

Perfect for individuals new to the field, small business owners, or anyone seeking to enhance their digital literacy and security awareness.

What You Will Learn

  • Define cybersecurity and its importance in the modern world.
  • Identify common cyber threats and attack vectors (malware, phishing, social engineering).
  • Understand basic security principles (CIA triad: Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability).
  • Learn best practices for password management and multi-factor authentication.
  • Explore network security fundamentals (firewalls, VPNs).
  • Grasp data privacy concepts and online safety tips.
  • Recognize different types of cyberattacks and how to react.
  • Discover potential career paths in cybersecurity.

About the Instructor
