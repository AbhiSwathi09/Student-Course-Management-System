Graphic Design Fundamentals

Master the principles of visual communication. Learn design theory, typography, color, and software essentials to create stunning visuals.

Instructor: Jane Smith

Enroll Now for Free!

Course Overview

Dive into the foundational concepts of graphic design. This course covers everything from understanding design principles like balance and contrast to hands-on practice with industry-standard software. You'll develop a keen eye for aesthetics and learn to convey powerful messages visually, preparing you for a creative career in design.

Perfect for aspiring designers, marketers, content creators, or anyone looking to enhance their visual communication skills.

What You Will Learn

  • Understand core design principles (alignment, proximity, contrast, repetition, hierarchy).
  • Master color theory and its application in design.
  • Learn typography basics: fonts, pairing, hierarchy, and legibility.
  • Create compelling layouts and compositions.
  • Introduction to graphic design software (e.g., Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator).
  • Develop a basic design portfolio.
